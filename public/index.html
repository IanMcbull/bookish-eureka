We provide a voice for the special needs community in Kenya

Rare Disorders Kenya is a community of people with rare diseases and all who support them. We provide a united voice for the rare disease community by capturing the experiences of patients and families and raising the profile of rare diseases across Kenya.

RARE DISEASES AT A GLANCE, GLOBALLY

  • Over 7,000 distinct rare diseases
  • 72% have Genetic origin and are chronic in nature – long term disabilities
  • 50% of rare diseases affect children, 30% don’t live past 5th birthday
  • Cumulatively affect 300 Million worldwide
  • Definition of Rare varies in different countries/regions: UK, America, Europe, WHO
